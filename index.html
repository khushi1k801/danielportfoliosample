About Me

Hi, I’m Daniel Whitmore — a wildlife photographer and conservationist dedicated to capturing the raw beauty of nature. My lens tells stories of endangered species, untamed landscapes, and the fragile balance of our planet. Through photography, I aim to inspire awareness, compassion, and action for wildlife conservation.
