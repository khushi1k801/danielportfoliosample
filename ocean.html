Beneath the Blue

Backstory

“Beneath the Blue” is an exploration of the hidden world under the ocean’s surface, where light, color, and life blend into an awe-inspiring symphony. This project captures the vibrant reefs, the fluid grace of marine creatures, and the breathtaking sense of calm that only the deep sea can bring.

Each photograph tells the story of an underwater kingdom, fragile yet resilient. From the tiniest coral polyp to the majestic sea turtle, this collection is a reminder of the beauty and importance of marine ecosystems. It also reflects the urgent call to protect these habitats from the threats of climate change and pollution.

The journey took me into the depths of tropical oceans, diving into vibrant coral gardens and drifting along with gentle currents. Every moment spent “beneath the blue” was a lesson in humility, showing how much more there is to discover beyond what we see from the surface.
