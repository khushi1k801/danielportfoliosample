The Flight of the Eagles

Backstory

“The Flight of the Eagles” is a tribute to the majesty and strength of one of nature’s most iconic birds of prey. Eagles have long symbolized freedom, power, and vision — soaring at heights where few dare to wander. This collection captures not just their grandeur in flight but also their intimate moments of survival, care, and instinct.

From breathtaking dives to tender nesting rituals, the project sheds light on the duality of the eagle — a fierce hunter and a nurturing parent. Shot across rugged cliffs and vast skies, these photographs invite viewers to witness the world from the eagle’s perspective — where the horizon is limitless.
